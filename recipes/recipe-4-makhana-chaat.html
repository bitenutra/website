Makhana Chaat

Makhana Chaat is a refreshing and nutritious snack made with crispy roasted fox nuts, fresh vegetables, and Indian spices. Perfect for evening cravings without guilt.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Makhana (Fox Nuts)
  • ½ cup chopped onions
  • ½ cup chopped tomatoes
  • ¼ cup chopped cucumber
  • ¼ cup chopped bell peppers (capsicum)
  • ¼ cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • ½ teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Optional: plain yogurt and sev for garnish

Method

  1. Dry roast makhana in a pan until crispy. Let them cool.
  2. In a large bowl, add roasted makhana and all chopped vegetables.
  3. Add lemon juice, olive oil, chaat masala, cumin powder, and salt.
  4. Toss everything gently until well mixed.
  5. Optional: add yogurt for a creamy version.
  6. Garnish with sev and extra chaat masala.
  7. Serve immediately for best taste and crunch.
