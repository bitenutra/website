Makhana Chaat

Dry roast Makhana until crispy. Mix roasted Makhana, onions, tomatoes, cucumber, bell peppers, coriander leaves, and green chili.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups Makhana
  • ½ cup onions
  • ½ cup tomatoes
  • ¼ cup cucumber
  • Chaat masala, lemon juice, olive oil

PROCESS

  1. Dry roast makhana until crispy.
  2. Add vegetables and spices.
  3. Toss well and serve immediately.
